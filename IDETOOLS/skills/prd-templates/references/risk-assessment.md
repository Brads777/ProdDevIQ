# Risk Assessment & Mitigation Plan

## Document Information
Record the product name, version, and date. Update this section each time the document is revised.

## Risk Matrix
Define a 3x3 or 5x5 matrix mapping probability (low/medium/high) against impact to determine risk levels.

## 1. Technical Risks

### Risk Entry Format
For each risk, document: description, probability, impact, mitigation strategy, and owner. Identify risks based on the technical architecture, infrastructure choices, and security considerations discussed with the user.

## 2. Business Risks

### Risk Entry Format
For each risk, document: description, probability, impact, mitigation strategy, and owner. Assess risks to revenue, market positioning, and stakeholder relationships based on the user's business context.

## 3. Project Risks

### Risk Entry Format
For each risk, document: description, probability, impact, mitigation strategy, and owner. Evaluate timeline, scope, budget, and resource constraints by asking the user about their delivery expectations.

## 4. Compliance Risks

### Risk Entry Format
For each risk, document: description, probability, impact, mitigation strategy, and owner. Determine applicable regulations and industry standards based on the user's domain and target markets.

## 5. Risk Response Actions

### Immediate Actions
List high-priority actions for critical risks. Ask the user which risks need urgent attention within the current sprint.

### Short-Term Actions
Identify important mitigations to complete within the quarter based on risk severity.

### Long-Term Actions
Outline strategic actions for ongoing risk management throughout the project lifecycle.

## 6. Risk Review Process

### Monthly Risk Review
Specify a cadence for reviewing open risks, updating assessments, and identifying new risks.

### Quarterly Risk Assessment
Plan comprehensive re-evaluations including external scanning and stakeholder input.

## Appendix: Risk Register Summary
Compile a reference table of all risks with their level, owner, and current status.
