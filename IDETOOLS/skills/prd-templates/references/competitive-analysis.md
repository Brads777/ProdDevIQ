# Competitive Analysis Template

## Executive Summary
Synthesize key findings after completing all sections. Summarize the competitive landscape and strategic recommendations.

## 1. Market Overview
Ask the user about their target market and industry before researching.

### 1.1 Market Size & Growth
Research market size, growth rate, and projections using industry reports and public data.

### 1.2 Key Market Trends
Identify 3-5 trends shaping the market. Ask the user which trends align with their product vision.

## 2. Competitor Analysis
Ask the user to name known competitors, then research to identify additional players.

### 2.1 Direct Competitors
Profile each competitor's positioning, strengths, weaknesses, and recent moves.

### 2.2 Feature Comparison Matrix
Create a comparison table of key features across all identified competitors.

### 2.3 Pricing Comparison
Research and tabulate pricing tiers, models, and positioning for each competitor.

## 3. SWOT Analysis (Our Product)
Conduct this analysis based on the user's product description and your competitor research.

### Strengths
Identify internal advantages based on the user's product capabilities and team.

### Weaknesses
Ask the user about limitations, then assess gaps relative to competitors.

### Opportunities
Identify external factors the product can capitalize on based on market research.

### Threats
Analyze external risks from competitors, market shifts, or regulatory changes.

## 4. Differentiation Strategy
Synthesize findings to define how the product stands apart.

### 4.1 Primary Differentiators
Recommend 2-3 key differentiators based on competitor gaps and user strengths.

### 4.2 Target Market Positioning
Define market positioning relative to competitors. Ask the user about target customer segments.

## 5. Go-to-Market Strategy
Develop actionable plans based on differentiation and target market.

### 5.1 Launch Phases
Propose a phased launch approach with milestones. Confirm timeline with the user.

### 5.2 Marketing Channels
Recommend prioritized channels based on target audience and competitor strategies.

### 5.3 Key Metrics for Success
Define measurable KPIs tied to competitive goals. Ask the user about their success criteria.

## 6. Competitive Response Plan
Anticipate competitor reactions and prepare contingencies.

### 6.1 If Competitors Lower Prices
Recommend response strategies for pricing pressure scenarios.

### 6.2 If Competitors Add Similar Features
Recommend strategies to maintain differentiation if competitors achieve feature parity.

### 6.3 If Major Player Enters Market
Recommend defensive strategies against well-funded new entrants.

## 7. Competitive Intelligence Process
Establish ongoing monitoring recommendations.

### 7.1 Monitoring Activities
Recommend regular activities to track competitor movements.

### 7.2 Sources
List reliable sources for competitive intelligence in this market.

### 7.3 Response Protocol
Define a standard process for acting on competitive intelligence updates.

## Appendix: Competitor Profiles
Create quick-reference cards for each competitor with key stats and positioning.
